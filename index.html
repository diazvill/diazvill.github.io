Interactive Projects

LMI Energy Burden in Tribal Nations

Energy burdens faced by low- and moderate income (LMI) American Indian, Alaska Native, and Native Hawaiian households. The highest burdens are concentrated in Alaska due to high energy consumption, reliance on diesel generators, and limited grid connectivity.

Energy Burden in Mississippi

This choropleth map shows county-level energy burden in Mississippi, highlighting areas where households spend a higher share of income on energy. It reveals regional disparities and identifies vulnerable communities.
